What Is a B2B Survey Panel?

Definition of B2B Survey Panel

A business-to-business survey panel comprises business professionals, executives, managers, and subject-matter experts who have been recruited and screened beforehand to engage in market research surveys conducted by firms looking for specific participants. In contrast to general consumer panels, B2B panels undergo screening based on industry, company size, occupation and tenure. Every piece of information received through such a panel comes from an expert opinion rather than a general participant.
